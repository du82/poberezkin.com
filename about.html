About

I am the founder of SimpleX Chat - the first (and the only?) messaging and application platform operating without user identifiers of any kind, not even random numbers are used to identify user profiles - it is 100% private by design!

SimpleX Chat is the next generation of decentralized communications, NOT based on crypto-currencies for a change. You can start using our private messaging apps for console, iOS and Android today - install the app, join our Mastodon or Reddit community and support us with donations!

I also created Ajv data validation library - it is used by millions of JavaScript applications and has ~350m monthly downloads on npm.
